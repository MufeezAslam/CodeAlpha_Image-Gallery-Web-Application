Image diagnostic — quick check

1) Local inline SVG (always should show)
2) Placeholder (needs internet)
3) Unsplash dynamic (needs internet & not blocked)

If 1) Inline SVG shows but 2) and/or 3) do not, your computer/browser cannot reach the internet or those hosts are blocked. If nothing shows, check the browser console (press F12 → Console) and copy any errors.
